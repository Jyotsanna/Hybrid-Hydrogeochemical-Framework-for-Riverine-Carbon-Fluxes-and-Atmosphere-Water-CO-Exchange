# References

## Overview

This document lists the primary scientific references, analytical standards, and software resources that support the hydrochemical analyses, statistical methods, and geochemical interpretations presented in this repository. These publications provide the theoretical and methodological foundation for evaluating water quality, hydrogeochemical processes, and riverine carbon dynamics.

---

# Water Quality and Hydrochemistry

### American Public Health Association (APHA)

APHA. (2017). *Standard Methods for the Examination of Water and Wastewater* (23rd ed.). American Public Health Association, American Water Works Association, Water Environment Federation.

**Purpose**

* Laboratory analysis of water samples
* Standard analytical procedures
* Water quality assessment

---

### Hem, J. D. (1985)

Hem, J. D. (1985). *Study and Interpretation of the Chemical Characteristics of Natural Water*. U.S. Geological Survey Water-Supply Paper 2254.

**Purpose**

* Hydrochemical interpretation
* Major ion chemistry
* Natural water processes

# Hydrochemical Classification
